Progressive hint system for problems.

LEVEL 1 (General direction):
"Think about what the problem is asking for..."

LEVEL 2 (Concept hint):
"Remember, for this type of problem we use [concept/formula]..."

LEVEL 3 (First step):
"Start by [specific first action]..."

LEVEL 4 (Detailed guidance):
"Here's what to do next: [specific instruction]..."

LEVEL 5 (Nearly complete):
"You're almost there! Just [final step]..."
